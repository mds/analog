Getting Started

Before your cameras arrive, get a head start and setup your asset(s) and thermal camera connection profile.

Connect Your Cameras

You’ll need the iOS app to connect your cameras to your account. Refer to app’s onscreen instructions to setup your cameras.

An Asset and a Camera Profile is required to connect the thermal cameras to your account. You can do this from the app, but it may be more efficient to setup that beforehand.

Create New Asset

Each camera must be added to an asset. Assets are the pieces of equipment that your camera(s) will monitor.

Create a Network Profile

Network profiles tell your thermal cameras which Wi-fi network and password to use for cloud connection.

Invite Others to Your Team

Each Asset can have multiple contacts assigned to receive notifications for detected heat anomalies.
